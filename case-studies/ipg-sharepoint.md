# Case Study: Enterprise Platform Consolidation

> **Industry**: Marketing/Healthcare  
> **Duration**: 4 years  
> **Role**: Director of Production & Technology

## Context

A major healthcare marketing agency needed to consolidate disparate technology platforms while scaling operations across $51M in annual project portfolio serving 13 pharmaceutical brands.

## Problem

- Fragmented technology stack across brands
- Manual processes causing delays
- On-premises infrastructure limiting scale
- Growing compliance requirements (HIPAA, SOX)

## Approach

1. **Standardization**: Established technology standards across brands
2. **Cloud Migration**: Azure-based platform with 99.9% SLA
3. **Automation**: CI/CD pipelines, automated testing
4. **Governance**: Compliance framework and audit readiness

## Outcomes

- **99.9% uptime** achieved post-migration
- **60% infrastructure cost reduction**
- **Zero HIPAA violations** across 4+ years
- Scaled team from **5 to 60+** engineers

## What I Would Productize

- Healthcare marketing platform reference architecture
- Brand-agnostic compliance framework
- Pharmaceutical content management patterns
